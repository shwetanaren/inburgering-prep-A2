# Inburgering Prep A2 - Simple Guide

This page is for people who want to use the app, not build it.

Live site: [https://shwetanaren.github.io/](https://shwetanaren.github.io/)

## What is this?

This is a simple learning app for people preparing for Dutch A2 inburgering.
It helps with:

- Dutch words
- sentence patterns
- short dialogues

The app is organized by themes, for example:

- supermarket
- municipality
- housing
- school
- work
- transport

## What can I do in the app?

You can:

- choose a theme
- learn Dutch words
- practice sentence building
- read and practice dialogues
- track your daily progress and streak

## Do I need to sign up?

No.

At the moment, the app works without an account.
Your progress is saved in your own browser on your own device.

## Important to know

Your progress is stored locally.
That means:

- it stays on the device/browser you used
- if you clear browser data, your progress may be lost
- if you switch to another phone or browser, your progress will not move with you yet

## Is this finished?

No. This is still an MVP.
More themes, more polish, and better offline support are planned.

## Can I share feedback?

Yes.

If something is confusing, broken, or missing, please open an issue on GitHub or message the project owner with:

- what you were trying to do
- what happened instead
- which phone/browser you used
